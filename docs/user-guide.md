# Shree Hindutakht User Guide

## Overview

This guide provides instructions for using the Shree Hindutakht community platform. The platform offers features for both regular community members and administrators to engage with the community, share content, participate in events, and manage the platform.

## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Visit the website homepage
2. Click on the "Register" button
3. Fill in your personal information:
   - Full name
   - Email address
   - Phone number
   - Address
   - Password (minimum 6 characters)
   - Confirm password
4. Click "Register"
5. You will be automatically logged in and redirected to your dashboard

### Logging In

1. Visit the website homepage
2. Click on the "Login" button
3. Enter your email and password
4. Click "Login"
5. You will be redirected to your dashboard

### Password Recovery

If you forget your password:
1. Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for a password reset link
4. Click the link and set a new password

## Member Features

### Dashboard

The dashboard is your main hub for accessing all platform features. From here you can:
- View the community feed
- Check upcoming events
- Make donations
- Access your profile
- View notifications

### Community Feed

The community feed displays posts from other members and administrators:

1. **Viewing Posts**: Scroll through the feed to see recent posts
2. **Creating Posts**: 
   - Click the "Create Post" button
   - Enter your message
   - Optionally add images (up to 5)
   - Click "Post"
3. **Interacting with Posts**:
   - Like posts by clicking the heart icon
   - Comment on posts by clicking the comment icon
   - Share posts by clicking the share icon

### Events

Stay updated with community events:

1. **Viewing Events**: Click on "Events" in the navigation to see all events
2. **RSVP to Events**: 
   - Click on an event to see details
   - Click "Interested" or "Going" to RSVP
   - Your response will be updated in real-time

### Profile Management

Manage your personal information:

1. **View Profile**: Click on your profile picture or name in the header
2. **Edit Profile**: 
   - Click "Edit Profile" from the profile menu
   - Update your information
   - Upload a new profile photo
   - Click "Save Changes"
3. **Change Password**:
   - Click "Change Password" from the profile menu
   - Enter your current password
   - Enter and confirm your new password
   - Click "Change Password"

### ID Card

Access your digital membership card:

1. Click on "ID Card" from the profile menu
2. Your digital ID card will be displayed
3. You can show this card at community events

### Notifications

Stay informed about community activities:

1. Click the notification bell icon in the header
2. View your recent notifications
3. Click on a notification to view details

### Donations

Support community initiatives:

1. Click on "Donations" in the navigation
2. View donation options
3. Select an amount or enter a custom amount
4. Follow the payment process

## Admin Features

### Admin Login

Administrators access the platform through a separate login:

1. Visit `/admin/login`
2. Enter admin credentials
3. Click "Login"

### Admin Dashboard

The admin dashboard provides an overview of platform activity:

1. **Statistics**: View total members, posts, and events
2. **Recent Activity**: See recent posts and member activity
3. **Quick Actions**: Access member management, post creation, and event management

### Member Management

Manage community members:

1. **View Members**: Click "Members" in the admin navigation
2. **Search Members**: Use the search bar to find specific members
3. **Edit Member**: 
   - Click on a member's name or the edit icon
   - Update member information
   - Click "Save Changes"
4. **Delete Member**: 
   - Click the delete icon next to a member
   - Confirm deletion

### Post Management

Manage community posts:

1. **View Posts**: Click "Posts" in the admin navigation
2. **Create Post**: 
   - Click "Create New Post"
   - Enter post content
   - Optionally add images
   - Click "Publish Post"
3. **Edit Post**: 
   - Click on a post or the edit icon
   - Update post content
   - Click "Save Changes"
4. **Delete Post**: 
   - Click the delete icon on a post
   - Confirm deletion

### Event Management

Manage community events:

1. **View Events**: Click "Events" in the admin navigation
2. **Create Event**: 
   - Click "Create New Event"
   - Enter event details (title, description, date, location)
   - Set event status (upcoming/past)
   - Click "Create Event"
3. **Edit Event**: 
   - Click on an event or the edit icon
   - Update event details
   - Click "Save Changes"
4. **Delete Event**: 
   - Click the delete icon on an event
   - Confirm deletion

### Donation Management

Track community donations:

1. **View Donations**: Click "Donations" in the admin navigation
2. **View Donation Records**: See all donation transactions
3. **Export Data**: Export donation records for reporting

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues and Solutions

#### Login Issues
- **Problem**: Cannot log in
- **Solution**: 
  1. Check your email and password
  2. Reset your password if needed
  3. Clear browser cache and cookies
  4. Try a different browser

#### Post Creation Issues
- **Problem**: Cannot create posts or upload images
- **Solution**: 
  1. Check file size limits (maximum 5MB per image)
  2. Ensure file formats are supported (JPG, PNG, GIF)
  3. Check internet connection
  4. Refresh the page and try again

#### Event RSVP Issues
- **Problem**: RSVP not updating
- **Solution**: 
  1. Refresh the page
  2. Check internet connection
  3. Try clicking the button again

#### Mobile App Issues
- **Problem**: App crashes or doesn't load
- **Solution**: 
  1. Restart the app
  2. Clear app cache
  3. Uninstall and reinstall the app
  4. Ensure you have the latest version

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: How do I reset my password?
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions sent to your email.

### Q: Can I change my email address?
A: Yes, you can update your email address in your profile settings.

### Q: How do I delete my account?
A: Contact the administrator through the contact form to request account deletion.

### Q: Are my photos private?
A: Photos you upload to your profile are visible to other community members. Posts are public within the community.

### Q: How often are events updated?
A: Events are updated regularly by administrators. You can check the events section for the latest information.

### Q: Can I use the platform on multiple devices?
A: Yes, you can access the platform from any device with a web browser or through the mobile app.

### Q: Is there a mobile app?
A: Yes, there is an Android app available for download.

### Q: How do I report inappropriate content?
A: Click the report button on any post or contact the administrator directly.
